Introduction

Welcome to TrendingTools. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of any information you provide while using our services. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information on our website.

Important: TrendingTools operates as a client-side only application. All tools and features run entirely in your browser, and we do not collect, store, or process any personal data.

Information We Do Not Collect

Since TrendingTools is a client-side application:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.
  • No Usage Data: We do not track your usage patterns, browsing history, or tool preferences.
  • No Location Data: We do not collect or store your geographical location or IP address.
  • No Cookies for Tracking: We do not use cookies for tracking purposes (see our Cookie Policy for details).
  • No Data Storage: All tool operations happen in your browser. We don't store any data on our servers.

Third-Party Services

Hosting Provider

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect certain technical information such as IP addresses for security and operational purposes. Please refer to GitHub's Privacy Policy for more information.

Font and Icon Services

We use Google Fonts and Font Awesome for typography and icons. These services may collect limited data about font usage. You can review their privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Font Awesome Privacy Policy

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, you have no data to request access to, correct, or delete. However, you have the right to:

  1. Use our tools anonymously without providing any personal information
  2. Disable JavaScript in your browser (though this will break functionality)
  3. Clear your browser's local storage and cache at any time
  4. Use browser extensions to block third-party requests

Children's Privacy

Our services are suitable for users of all ages. Since we don't collect personal information, we are fully compliant with children's privacy regulations including COPPA.

Data Security

While we implement security best practices in our code, the ultimate security of your data depends on:

  • Your browser's security settings
  • Your device's security measures
  • Your network security

We recommend keeping your browser updated and using security extensions for optimal protection.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Posting a notice on our website

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact Us page.
