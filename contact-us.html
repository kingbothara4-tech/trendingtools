Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about TrendingTools.

Are your tools really free to use?

Yes! All 10 tools on TrendingTools are completely free to use. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or premium tiers. We believe in providing valuable tools without charging our users.

Do you store any of my data?

No. All tools operate client-side in your browser. We don't collect, store, or process any of your data on our servers. Your privacy is our priority.

Can I use these tools for commercial purposes?

Yes, you can use our tools for both personal and commercial purposes. However, please review our Terms of Service for specific usage restrictions and requirements.

Do I need to create an account?

No account required! You can use all tools immediately without any registration. No emails, no passwords, no personal information needed.

How can I report a bug or issue?

You can report bugs by emailing kingbothara4@gmail.com or using the contact form above. Please include details about the issue, what browser you're using, and steps to reproduce the problem.

Can I suggest new tools or features?

Absolutely! We welcome feature requests and tool suggestions. Email us at kingbothara4@gmail.com or use the contact form. We review all suggestions and consider them for future updates.
