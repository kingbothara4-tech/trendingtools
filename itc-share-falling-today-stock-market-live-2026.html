Many people are asking: "Why ITC share is falling today?" Let me explain in simple words.

ITC is a big company. It makes cigarettes, hotels, and food. Today, ITC share price is going down. The price is around ‚Çπ432.50. This is 3.2% lower than yesterday.

üí° Simple Fact: When many people sell a company's shares, the price goes down. When many people buy, the price goes up. Today, more people are selling ITC shares.

üìä Main Reasons for ITC Share Price Fall

Here are the main reasons why ITC share is falling today:

  1. Government tax news - Government may increase tax on cigarettes. This is bad for ITC because they sell many cigarettes.
  2. Market sentiment - Today, whole stock market is a little down. When market falls, many shares fall too.
  3. Profit booking - Some people bought ITC shares earlier at low price. Now they are selling to take profit.
  4. Foreign investors selling - Foreign companies are selling ITC shares today.
  5. Competition news - Other companies are making similar products. This can affect ITC sales.
